Toasted PB&J

A simple recipe to warm up one of your favorite childhood meals!

Prep Time: 2 minutes

Cook Time: 2 - 3 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Softened stick butter
  • Penut butter
  • Jelly (which ever you like)

Directions

  1. Toast the two slices of bread.

  2. Lightly spread butter on one side

  3. On the other side apply peanut butter and jelly

  4. Combine the bread to make the sandwich

  5. Enjoy!
